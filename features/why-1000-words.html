Why 1,000 Words?

The magic number that unlocks Japanese conversation

The Science Behind 1,000 Words

Research shows that knowing the most frequent 1,000 words in any language gives you approximately 75-80% comprehension of everyday conversation. This isn't just theory – it's the foundation that makes real communication possible.

Quality Over Quantity

Instead of overwhelming you with thousands of random vocabulary words, WordSen focuses on the essential 1,000 that appear most frequently in:

  • Daily conversations with friends and family
  • Basic business and workplace interactions
  • Shopping, dining, and travel situations
  • News articles and social media
  • Anime, manga, and popular culture

Build Momentum, Not Overwhelm

With a clear, achievable goal of 1,000 words, you can:

  • Track your progress with meaningful milestones
  • Feel confident as you recognize more and more Japanese
  • Build a solid foundation for advanced learning
  • Actually use what you've learned in real situations
